Registration Details

Refer to the event you have register into below

Autumn 5-aside

Basics

  • 1. At least one player must pay their player fee one week prior to the beginning date to secure your team a spot.
  • 2. Players with a 5sports Competition Pass may be entitled to register for free.
  • 3. If payment is not received your team may lose their place in the competition.
  • 4. All players must be registered and paid in full before Round One of the competition to become eligible to play.
  • All 5sports Terms and Conditions apply.

Instructions for Team Captain / Manager

  • 1. Forward your Captain/Manager Email to each player (or parent if juniors).
  • 2. Ask them to click the Join Team link in that email.
  • 3. Follow the steps to pay the registration fee.
  • 4. They will receive an emailed receipt after payment.
  • Monitor your player registrations and payments Here

Shire Shield

Basics

  • 1. The Team Manager (or parent of the team captain) must pay the full team registration fee.
  • 2. If not paid by April 5th 2026 your team may lose their place.
  • 3. All players must be registered before the event day.
  • 4. All 5sports Terms and Conditions apply.

Instructions for Team Manager

  • 1. Forward your Manager Email to the parents of players in your team.
  • 2. Ask them to click the Join Team link.
  • 3. Follow the steps in the portal and complete the $0 registration payment.
  • Monitor your player registrations and payments Here
  • Booking Fees Apply: A 1.95% Booking Fee is applied by our booking software provider at the checkout to all transactions.
